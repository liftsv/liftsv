Overview

We have the potential to dramatically shape culture by helping steward the gifts of the Bay Area technical creative community. Through partnering with churches and organizations, we provide opportunities for like-minded professionals to network, learn, grow and be discipled; all while building innovative Kingdom-minded technologies.

Pillars

Convene

Building community by establishing multiple gathering opportunities.

Engage

Equiping leaders for discipleship and the stewardship of their gifts.

Pastor

Empowering relational prayer, care and spiritual development.

Partners

Silicon Valley is a unique region with an incredibly high number of technical creatives in our various congregations. Not every church could or should hire a Pastor of Innovation to serve these communities. Partnering with LIFT Ministries provides a simple, method for engaging members into the body of Christ using their special talents.

A virtual presence of each partner church’s content is utilized in small group gatherings. We also provide all of the necessary marketing materials needed to promote these opportunities to church members, and develop key volunteer leaders in each region to represent their respective communities.

Ministries

Gather

Monthly community events offering technical creatives a place to network with other Christians and learn from each other through testimony, product demos and guest speakers.

Equip

Six week training workshops that form digital missional small groups and equip participants with the operational and discipleship skills for stewarding their technical gifting.

Build

Intensive weekend where developers, programmers, designers, creatives and entrepreneurs work together to build new Kingdom-minded technologies that serve our communities.

Grow

Providing one-on-one mentorship for leaders and guiding the placement or formation of people into regular workplace, affinity or regional based prayer groups for bible studies and discipleship.
