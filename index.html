Overview

We have the potential to dramatically shape culture by helping steward the gifts of the Bay Area technical creative community. Through partnering with churches and organizations, we will provide opportunities for like-minded professionals to network, learn, grow and be discipled; all while building innovative Kingdom-minded technologies.

Pillars

Convene

Build community by establishing multiple gathering opportunities.

Engage

Equip leaders for discipleship and the stewardship of their gifts.

Pastor

Empower relational prayer, care and spiritual development.

Partners

We live in a unique region with an incredibly high number of technical creatives in our congregations. Not every church could or should hire a Pastor of Innovation to serve these communities. Partnering with LIFT Ministries provides a simple, affordable, method for engaging members into the body of Christ using their special talents.

We integrate a virtual presence of each partner church’s content to be utilized in small group gatherings. We will also provide all of the necessary marketing materials needed to promote these opportunities to church members, and develop key volunteer leaders in each region to represent their respective communities.

Community

Gather

Monthly community events offer technical creatives a place to network with other Christians and learn from each other through testimony, product demos and guest speakers.

Equip

Six week training workshops that form digital missional small groups and equip participants with the operational and discipleship skills for stewarding their technical gifting.

Build

Intensive weekend where developers, programmers, designers, creatives and entrepreneurs work around the clock to build new Kingdom-minded technologies that serve our communities.

Grow

Will provide 1:1 mentorship for leaders and guide the formation or placement of people into regular workplace, affinity or regional based prayer groups for bible studies and discipleship.

Get Involved

Serving as the Pastor of Innovation for Venture Christian Church in Los Gatos for the past two years, Marc Krejci has directed and led efforts to activate the technology and entrepreneur community in order to steward their talents towards Kingdom initiatives, while simultaneously guiding digital projects internal to church operations.
